# Sprint 8: Ecosystem, Sync & Polish

**Goal:** Finalize the app as a complete ecosystem with data portability and cross-device sync.

## Features
- **Cloud Sync (WebDAV/Cloud)**: Sync reading position, collections, and highlights across devices using user-owned storage (No proprietary Sumi cloud).
- **Import/Export**: Full library backup and export of annotations to Markdown, JSON, or Notion.
- **Reading Reports**: Advanced statistics (Reading streaks, charts, heatmaps).
- **Final UI/UX Polish**: Shared element transitions, motion design, and refined icons.
- **App Packaging**: Final builds for Android, iOS, Windows, and Linux.
