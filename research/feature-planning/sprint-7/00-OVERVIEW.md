# Sprint 7: Power User & AI (OpenRouter Integration)

**Goal:** Leverage AI and library automation for advanced research and discovery.

## Features
- **OpenRouter Integration**:
    - **Summarization**: Generate summaries of chapters or highlights.
    - **Q&A**: "Ask your book" - semantic search and context-aware chat.
    - **Key Terms**: Auto-extract definitions for complex terms.
- **OPDS Support**: Connect to Calibre libraries, Project Gutenberg, and Standard Ebooks directly from the app.
- **External Dictionaries**: Support for StarDict or online lookups (Wiktionary, etc.).
- **Automatic Library Scanning**: Watch folders and auto-import new books with metadata cleanup.
